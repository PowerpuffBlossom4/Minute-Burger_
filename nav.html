ABOUT US

Welcome to Minute Burger, where we been serving delicious, freshly-made burgers with a commitment to quality and affordability since 1982. With over four decades of experience, Minute Burger has become a beloved name in the fast-food industry, known for our iconic burgers that satisfy cravings anytime, anywhere.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide tasty, affordable, and convenient meals to our customers. Today, we have grown into a trusted brand with hundreds of stores nationwide, each dedicated to delivering the same great taste and service that has made us a favorite among burger lovers.

At Minute Burger, we pride ourselves on using high-quality ingredients, from our freshly-baked buns to our savory patties, all prepared with care to ensure every bite is delicious. Whether youre grabbing a quick snack or a hearty meal, our diverse menu offers something for everyone, including our classic burgers, chicken sandwiches, and delightful sides.

Community and customer satisfaction are at the heart of what we do. We continuously strive to innovate and improve our offerings while maintaining the exceptional value that our customers have come to expect. Our friendly staff, fast service, and commitment to excellence ensure that every visit to Minute Burger is a memorable experience..
